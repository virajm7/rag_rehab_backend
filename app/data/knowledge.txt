Clinic Overview
Reviva Rehab Clinic is a physiotherapy and rehabilitation center dedicated to restoring mobility, reducing pain, and improving overall wellness. 
The clinic is located in Bengaluru, India and provides personalized treatment plans for patients of all ages.

Services Offered
1. Physiotherapy (Musculoskeletal & Orthopedic Care)
2. Post-Surgery Rehabilitation
3. Neurological Rehabilitation (Stroke, Parkinson’s, Spinal Cord Injury)
4. Sports Injury Rehabilitation
5. Pediatric Therapy (Developmental Delays, Cerebral Palsy)
6. Spine Care Therapy (Back & Neck Pain)
7. Pain Management & Therapeutic Modalities
8. Geriatric Rehabilitation (Age-related Mobility Issues)
9. Home Visit Physiotherapy (On Appointment)
